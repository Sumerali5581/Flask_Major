  • TSUNAMI

    • Familiarize yourself with tsunami evacuation routes and the location of higher ground in your area.
    • If you are near the coast and feel an earthquake, move to higher ground immediately as a tsunami may follow.
    • Stay away from the coast after a tsunami warning until authorities declare it safe to return, as multiple waves may occur.
