  • EARTHQUAKE

    • Create an emergency kit with essential supplies such as water, food, first aid supplies, and flashlights.
    • Identify safe spots in your home or workplace, such as under sturdy furniture, and practice drop, cover, and hold-on drills regularly.
    • Secure heavy items such as bookshelves and water heaters to walls to prevent them from falling during an earthquake.
