  • LANDSLIDE

    • Be aware of signs of landslide activity, such as unusual sounds, slope cracking, or tilting trees.
    • Avoid building or living in areas prone to landslides, such as steep slopes or areas with poor drainage.
    • Plant ground cover on slopes and install retaining walls to help stabilize soil and prevent erosion.
